The Story
Every solution starts with identification of a problem. Want to know how we came up with this? Read on.
Why did we start this project?
When technology started to exist, it did because of one commmon goal - Technology for the greater good. Since childhood, we are being taught the principles of sustainable development and ways we can revolutionise the world and take care of our planet.
How did we implement our idea?
We used a CNN model to detect the soil present in the image uploaded by the farmer and according to the soil suggest the best crops. We used flask to integrate the model into our website.
Did we face any challenges?
Identification of the correct model and training and testing it with a machine of the bare minimum power took us a little longer than usual.
Who can use this tech?
Farmers and even people who wish to have an in-house garden at their place can utilise this software and get the best suggestions for their next produce.
